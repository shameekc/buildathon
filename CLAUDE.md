# Project: Hackathon Submission & Progress Tracker

## What We're Building

A tool for hackathon teams to submit progress updates and share their project with judges — solving the gap where teams have no structured way to do this during an event.

## Problem
At a hackathon, teams have no way to submit progress updates or share their project with judges.

## Target User
A team leader at 10:05 AM who needs to submit their idea before the deadline. They are under time pressure, arriving at the venue, and need to get their team registered and idea submitted fast.

## Success Criteria
Team created, idea submitted, and first update posted within 5 minutes of arriving.

## Core User Flows
1. Team leader creates a team
2. Submits their idea
3. Posts a first progress update

All three steps must be completable in under 5 minutes from a cold start.

## Design Principles
- Speed over polish — every screen must be fast to complete
- Mobile-first — team leaders will be on their phones
- Minimal friction — no unnecessary fields, no unnecessary steps
- Works under stress — hackathon morning is chaotic; the UX must be bulletproof simple

## Context
This is being built for a BITSAA vibe coding buildathon.
